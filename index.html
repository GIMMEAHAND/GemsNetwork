Questions

Everything you want to know.

What exactly do I get for $19/month?+
Access to a full library of beauty-specific digital products (e-books, guides, templates) you can sell with your unique affiliate link and earn 20% commission. Plus marketing swipe files, content calendars, training modules, Monday Night Live access and replays, and full community membership with mentorship from Ms. L.
Do I have to create any products?+
No. Every product is already built. You're selling existing products using your affiliate link. The marketing materials — captions, graphics, email templates — are done for you too. You customize and share. That's it.
I'm not techy. Can I still do this?+
Absolutely. The training walks you through everything step by step. If you can use Instagram, you can do this. The system is designed as training wheels — you learn as you earn, at your own pace, with a community behind you.
Is this an MLM?+
No. You're not buying inventory. You're not building a downline. You're earning commissions on digital products — the same way any affiliate program works. The difference is these products are built specifically for beauty pros, and you get the marketing, training, and community to support you. GEMS is a monetization community with affiliate benefits — not a network marketing company.
How much can I realistically earn?+
It depends on your effort and audience. One $27 product sale earns you $5.40 — that single sale nearly covers a third of your monthly membership. Five sales a week puts you at about $27/week or roughly $108/month in commissions. As your audience grows and your content strategy strengthens, that number scales. This is a system, not a lottery ticket.
I don't have a big audience. Will this work?+
You don't need 10,000 followers. You need people who trust you — and as a salon professional, you already have that. Your clients, your peers, your community. The training teaches you how to grow from there using Pinterest, TikTok, Instagram, and live selling.
What's the difference between Ruby and Diamond?+
Ruby ($19/month) is where every member starts — you get the full product library, affiliate links, training, marketing tools, and community. It's everything you need to learn the system and start earning at 20% commission. Diamond ($49/month) is an upgrade for members who've learned the system and want to build their own brand — you get 35% commissions, white-label rights to rebrand and sell products as your own, referral bonuses, and advanced training. You earn your way to Diamond — it's not a starting point.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. No contracts, no commitments. Cancel anytime. But once the founding rate ends, new members pay $29/month. If you cancel and rejoin later, you'll rejoin at the current price — not the founding rate.
